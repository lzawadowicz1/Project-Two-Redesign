There are many opportunities to volunteer within the Riverside Art Center organization. If you would like to help RAC, please see the below list of items that can use your assistance:
1. Daily Hours: Keeping the gallery open to the public during set hours is a vital part of our organization. The more volunteers we have, the more times the gallery can be open to the public.
2. Refreshments: for members meetings and specific shows.
3. Join a committee! Each event that RAC hosts requires much detail and many hands to help- from the smallest of detail such as setting up tables and chairs, to taking photos at the event, or even greeting the public at the door, RAC encourages members to get involved and be a part of the organization!
4. Inventory your skills: Please fill out the skills survey. It will help with ideas of what you might do as a volunteer and if helps us identify who might help with various activities.
If you feel you can contribute to any of the above items (we also take suggestions), please contact RAC at 419-738-2352, or email us at: riversideartcenter@gmail.com
